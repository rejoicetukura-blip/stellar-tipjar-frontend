# Stellar Tip Jar Marketing Guide

## Brand Overview
- Name: Stellar Tip Jar
- Tone: Friendly, empowering, creator-first.
- Colors: #3B82F6 (primary blue), #1F2937 (dark), #FBBF24 (accent), #FFFFFF (white)
- Typography: Inter, system sans-serif fallback.

## Design Structure
- `design/marketing/` for source assets and templates.
- `design/email-templates/` for transactional/marketing emails.
- `public/og-images/` for Open Graph and social sharing images.

## Social Media Assets
- Profile icons and cover images should be square and high-DPI.
- Use consistent logo, colors, and messaging.

## Email Assets
- Provide responsive HTML + plain-text.
- Keep CTA prominent and single-action focused.

## Presentation Materials
- Pitch deck: 15-20 slides.
- One-pager: quick product summary.

## Additional Assets
- Infographics that explain tipping flow and creator metrics.
- Event banners for campaigns.

## Usage
- Always refer to brand color/typography section.
- Export assets with proper format and optimization (SVG/PNG/WebP).
